Initial Model File Setup

This demonstration does not require an installation script nor are there any global symbols pre-defined.

BUT it does require you to do the following :-

(1) Pick Single Line, MB3, Navigator, turn off Linear and Radial/Diameter auto dimension.

(2) Pick Lighting under Shaded Hardware, turn off Observer lighting and turn on Left Shoulder.

(3) Pick Options under Shaded Hardware, Hardware Support... , and turn hardware backlighting on.

(4) Also set the Shaded Options from Normal to Fine.

(5) Ensure that the Auto Draw is turned on under Options, Preferences, Display.
