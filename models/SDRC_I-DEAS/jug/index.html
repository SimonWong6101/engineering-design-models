This is designed as a quick combined surfacing and solid modeling demo. As the part is constructed solid modeling type commands are used such as Join and Shell, and surfacing type commands are also used such as Loft and Sweep. The resultant part has full history and the AE can add more, for example the sweep center line could have some through points constrained. Thus the AE can make dimensional changes and free form changes on the handle sweep center line and the ellipse.

No Start model files or program files are required.

Thanks to Neill Butcher in the UK for the original demo.
