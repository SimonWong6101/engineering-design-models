STEP_NAME= STEP 1: Assemble DESCRIPTION= 1. Remove burrs from holes on both ends in recess area. Plug Gage Go 9804. 2. Wire brush ID thread. 3. Degrease in vapor stage for 45 seconds and spray with lance. 4. Immerse in a non-etch alloy alkaline cleaner at 200 degrees F for 10 minutes. 5. Rinse in cold water for 20 seconds. 6. Electroless Nickel plate in an enplate NI400 or Shipley 224 solution at 300 degrees to required thickness. TIME= 25 MINS
