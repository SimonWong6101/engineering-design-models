Powerful NC Programming Functionality

These toolpaths machine the four countersunk screw hole locations on the core. The holes cannot be simply drilled because they are drafted. For most systems, machining these areas efficiently is difficult. We easily machine these areas by creating a helical cutline toolpath. The cutter follows a continuous helix from the top of the hole to the bottom and is engaged in the material at all times. As a result, we completely finish machines these surfaces including a full clean up pass at the bottom depth.
