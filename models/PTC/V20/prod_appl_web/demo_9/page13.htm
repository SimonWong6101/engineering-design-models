NC Programming

Unmatched NC Programming Capabilities

The NC programming tools provide tremendous functionality for generating toolpaths for milling, turning, WEDM, and sheetmetal. Within the milling environment, we provide tools for 2 through 5 axis machining including roughing, automatic clean up, edge or trajectory milling, flowline machining, swarf milling, holemaking, and complete tool axis control for multi-axis machining. Various cutter types are supported including: ball, flat, bull, and taper cutters, as well as form tools such as lollipop cutters and under cut tools. Entry conditions include: plunge outside of the workpiece, plunge through pre-drilled hole, ramp, helical, tangential, and radial. Although it is simple to generate toolpaths by merely selecting the geometry to machine and specifying the desired tool and operating parameters, the user also has complete customization control which allow him to graphically manipulate the toolpath at the most granular level.
