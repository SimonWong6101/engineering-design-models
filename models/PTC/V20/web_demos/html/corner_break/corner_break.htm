Pro/MFG Corner Breaks



Breaking sharp corners on parts while they are being machined saves costly time intensive deburring processes. Pro/MFG provides automatic and manual corner breaking for better part finishes.



Corner break
In the following example a finish toolpath profiling the outer surfaces of the part has been programmed with automatic corner radius breaks. No sharp corners are left because the burrs have been rolled (machined) off.




Corners
The "Corners" menu allows the setup of corner conditons, you may specify each individual corner or all corners automatically. Options for corners include concave, convex or chamfer. Corner radius or chamfer length may be setup ahead of time or prompted on the fly.
