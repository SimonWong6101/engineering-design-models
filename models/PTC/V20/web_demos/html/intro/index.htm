Manufacturing Solutions

Products and Solutions: Pro/CASTING Pro/CASTING

The high quality, close-tolerance dies and castings required by today's manufacturing industries are created quickly and easily with Pro/CASTING. Pro/CASTING's parametric, feature-based architecture permits numerous iterations of the tooling design early in the product-development process to optimize casting geometry, die cavities, and cores. As changes are made anywhere in the process, all deliverables update automatically -- resulting in faster time to market, lower development costs, and higher quality parts.

Products and Solutions: Pro/CMM Pro/CMM

Pro/CMM is used by Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) programmers to create inspection programs from Pro/ENGINEER design models. Using a graphical programming interface tightly integrated with Pro/ENGINEER, features can be measured by defining probe locations, feature tolerance information can be accessed directly from the Pro/ENGINEER model, and automatic collision detection is provided to ensure that expensive probe tooling clears the manufacturing assembly. With Pro/CMM, programmers can efficiently develop programs off-line from the CMM, ensuring a correct inspection program the first time around.

Products and Solutions: Pro/MFG Pro/MFG

Pro/MFG is a set of CAM solutions to generate highly efficient, production quality CNC tool paths. Capable of programming 2- through 5-axis milling, 2- through 5-axis turning with live tooling, 2- and 4-axis wire EDM, and 2-axis contourng, Pro/MFG uses the highly accurate and precise solid model developed in Pro/ENGINEER as a reference for developing NC tool paths. Integration with the Pro/ENGINEER product line allows programmers to design fixtures and to create setup and operation sheets. Pro/MFG's breadth of automated machining strategies makes it ideal for the mold making, automotive, aerospace, casting, and medical industries, or any shop with requirements for machining 2-D and 3D components.

Products and Solutions: Pro/MOLDESIGN Pro/MOLDESIGN

Pro/MOLDESIGN provides solutions for the design and manufacture of tooling for plastic molded parts -- including design, assembly, analysis, and documentation. As development teams work concurrently to develop the product and tool design, all deliverables and procedures update automatically reducing product costs, and overall time to market. Pro/MOLDESIGN's integration with third-party plastic flow analysis packages allows shops to analyze and optimize flow characteristics before cutting any steel. Multiple iterations of the design also result in a higher quality, incorporating the best principles of design-for-manufacturing.
For Release 18.0, shrinkage documentation enhancements allow users to show either a percent of shrinkage or final shrinkage-compensated dimensions. User productivity enhancements include check-mark blanking of Pro/MOLDESIGN surfaces and volumes. Mold based libraries have been enhanced by leveraging Pro/ENGINEER® simplified representation technology and offer three distinct levels of complexity, which include fully detailed models, simplified representations, and datum curves to indicate mold base boundaries. The mold section of Pro/LIBRARYTM will contain mold-specific geometry such as sprue, runner, and gate UDFs.

Products and Solutions: Pro/NC-CHECK Pro/NC-CHECK

Pro/NC-CHECK provides NC programmers with on-screen graphical simulation of CNC tool paths generated by Pro/MFG. The ability to visually verify an NC tool path enables the user to detect interferences and prove-out each aspect of cutting a part before valuable machine and operator time are committed. Multiple tool path iterations can then be quickly and easily explored -- and results immediately verified -- all within the Pro/ENGINEER environment. Integrated with Pro/MFG, no data translation is necessary to validate NC tool paths.

Products and Solutions: Pro/NC-POST Pro/NC-POST

Pro/NCPOST is an easy-to-use development environment that generates NC post-processors for any type of CNC machine, lowering NC post-processor development and maintenance costs. Pro/NCPOST uses a comprehensive, tree-structured questionnaire managed by an expert system to interactively describe a specific machine/control combination for each post-processor to be created. Pro/NCPOST is integrated with Pro/MFG to offer seamless conversion of cutter location data files to G and M codes

Products and Solutions: Pro/PROCESS for MFG Pro/PROCESS for MFG

Pro/PROCESS for MFG provides tools to generate step-by-step instructions describing the processes required to manufacture parts. Production-ready plans can be created including machining and non-machining steps, time/cost estimates, and graphical documentation of the manufacturing assembly, for release to the shop floor. And Pro/PROCESS for MFG's fully associative, parametric capabilities enable true concurrent development by allowing you to make changes to the product design and automatically update the process plan - significantly reducing our company's design-to-manufacturing cycle times.

Products and Solutions: Pro/SHEETMETAL Pro/SHEETMETAL

Pro/SHEETMETAL provides designers with specialized tools for the design and manufacture of sheet metal parts. These specialized design features include punches, notches, and forms, to capture manufacturing information in parametric 3-D models. The models can be automatically flattened and used to generate CNC programs, providing stock utilization and machine time utilization.
With Release 18.0, Pro/SHEETMETAL users now have the ability to create standard sheet metal sections and merge them into a single part. Tule-checking capability allows designers to check parts against company guidelines that govern the location and size of cuts, bends, and walls. These additons will enhance users' ability to reuse geometry, automate repetitive tasks, and simplify the design process, while delivering better, more manufacturable parts, shortening the overall product development cycle.

Products and Solutions: Pro/VERIFY Pro/VERIFY

Pro/VERIFY is a powerful analysis tool for comparing data gathered by an automated inspection machine, such as a laser scanner, to the Pro/ENGINEER solid model. Pro/VERIFY enables manufacturers to inspect parts produced in-house or by a supplier, by automatically comparing scanned point-cloud data to the Pro/ENGINEER model. This provides a highly accurate comparison of physical parts to theoretical design, resulting in reduced inspection time and design-to-manufacturing cycles for your company.
