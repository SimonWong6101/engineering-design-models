Manufacturing Solutions
 Seamless Integration of Design and Manufacturing

Design for Manufacturability

Pro/ENGINEER Manufacturing Solutions empower manufacturing engineers by encouraging their input early in the product-design process, leveraging their expertise through up-front involvement. This communication facilitates design for manufacturability based on proven practices, available resources, and time constraints -- improving productivity and enhancing product quality. Through extensive CNC capabilities -- including milling, hole-making, turning and wire electronic discharge machining (WEDM) -- Pro/ENGINEER Manufacturing Solutions provide tools that enable a company to reduce time to market and increase profitability.

Concurrent Product and Process Development

By leveraging a single database structure and cognitive features, Pro/ENGINEER Mechanical Design and Manufacturing Solutions enable engineers to implement change even late in the product development process, without prolonging time to market. This allows the utilization of in-process work and reduces overall cycle times. Plus, a single user interface permits design and manufacturing engineers to easily collaborate and leverage one another's knowledge -- reducing manufacturing lead times and improving productivity.
"Getting a design concept out of an engineer's head and into a mathematically precise shape is critical. Pro/ENGINEER's solid modeling helps us to do that quickly."

    Intelligent, Feature-Driven, Manufacturing Process Automation

    Pro/ENGINEER Manufacturing Solutions easily allow the expansion of a single design model into a family of products by leveraging "family table" capabilities. PTC's customers have found that using family tables can provide up to a 90% reduction in development time, while incorporating and automatically updating NC tool paths for each product rendition. This enables organizations to respond quickly to target markets worldwide by easily expanding product offerings. To further reduce cycle times, scrap and rework while improving quality, manufacuring engineers can graphically visualize progressive material removal to ensure the accuracy of toolpaths from the start.

    • Automatically carry out tool path instructions for machining strategies such as milling, hole-making, turning, and WEDM -- minimizing costly programming time and leveraging proven manufacturing processes.
    • Incorporate changes, even late in the development process, through the associativity between manufacturing features and the design model -- drastically reducing time spent completing Engineering Change Orders (ECOs).
    • Expand product offerings through powerful "family table" programming which automatically updates toolpaths for each rendition -- eliminating costly reprogramming and reusing field-tested engineering data.
    • Graphically view progressive material removal on screen, giving engineers the ability to verify toolpaths prior to cutting metal -- eliminating scrap and lowering overall costs.
    • Leverage a tightly integrated, best-in-class NC post processor which allows machine code to be generated seamlessly -- increasing accuracy and maintaining design intent.
