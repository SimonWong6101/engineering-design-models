Pro/ENGINEER Production Solutions

Web Enabled Toolpath Demos

Pro/ENGINEER Production Solutions provide NC programmers with unmatched flexibility, functionality, and accuracy in generating NC toolpaths for their NC machines. The single greatest benefit is derived from generating toolpaths directly on the Pro/ENGINEER solid model geometry. This results in:

    elimination of data translation
    guaranteed accuracy
    automatic updating of toolpaths to geometric modifications

yielding:

    decreased programming time
    decreased machine cycle time
    reduced scrap and rework
    higher surface finish and part quality

Functionality includes:

    2-5 axis milling
    2-4 axis turning
    mill/turn centers
    2-4 axis wire EDM
    sheetmetal fabrication
    integrated postprocessing

Pro/ENGINEER Production Solutions enable a company to reduce time to market and increase profitability.
